About the Application

Social Script Cards is an educational mobile application that provides pre-written social communication scripts organised by category, together with practice tools, sorting exercises, and situation-matching activities. The Application is designed to support individuals who benefit from structured social prompts, including people on the autism spectrum and those with social anxiety. The Application is intended for personal learning and practice. It is not a social network and does not enable communication between users.
