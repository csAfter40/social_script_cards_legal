Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Social Script Cards app for mobile devices, together with any related services operated by Muzaffer Barış Yavuz (collectively, the "Application"). Muzaffer Barış Yavuz is hereby referred to as the "Service Provider".


Information Collection and Use

The Application is designed to keep most of your information on your device. The Service Provider does not operate a backend that collects page views, session duration, or general usage analytics.

Information stored on your device

  • Profile information you choose to enter (such as first name, last name, and language preference)
  • App preferences (such as theme and accessibility settings)
  • Custom scripts you create, favorites, recently viewed scripts, and practice progress
  • Subscription status cached locally after verification
  • Optional photos you add to custom script steps (stored on your device)
  • Parental lock settings (PIN material is stored in secure storage on your device)

Information processed when you use certain features

  • Camera (practice mode only): If you enable the optional mirror, the camera preview is shown on your device only. The Application does not record or upload camera video.
  • Text-to-speech: Script text may be read aloud using your device's built-in speech engine; that processing occurs on your device.
  • Backup and restore: If you export a backup file, you choose where that file is saved or shared.

Information transmitted to third parties

  • Subscriptions (RevenueCat): When you subscribe, restore purchases, or check subscription status, subscription and transaction-related data is sent to RevenueCat to validate your entitlement. RevenueCat assigns an anonymous identifier; the Application does not send your profile name or email to RevenueCat. The Service Provider has not enabled RevenueCat integrations, webhooks, or hosted paywalls that would share your data with other third parties.
  • App stores (Apple App Store / Google Play): Payment processing and subscription management are handled by the store. The Service Provider does not receive your full payment card details.
  • Content updates: The Application may download updated script and situation content from a content delivery network. These requests do not include your profile, usage statistics, or personally identifying data beyond standard network connection metadata (such as IP address, which the hosting provider may log as part of normal HTTP delivery).
  • App updates and reviews: The Application may check store listings for updates and may prompt you to rate the app using your device's store services.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

The mobile Application does not use browser cookies. Third-party SDKs embedded in the Application (such as the subscription SDK) may use device identifiers and network requests as needed to provide their services. The Application does not use third-party advertising or cross-app tracking SDKs.

Third Party Access

The Service Provider does not sell your personal information. The Service Provider does not share your on-device profile, custom scripts, or usage statistics with third parties for advertising.

The Service Provider uses the following categories of service providers:

  • Subscription management (RevenueCat): Validates subscriptions and maintains anonymous customer records. RevenueCat may process data in the United States and other countries. See RevenueCat's privacy policy below.
  • Platform stores (Apple / Google): Process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • Content hosting: Delivers optional content updates (e.g., updated JSON assets).

Social Script Cards uses RevenueCat solely to manage in-app subscriptions. The app does not identify you to RevenueCat with your name or email. RevenueCat receives an anonymous customer identifier, subscription transaction data from Apple or Google, and basic device and app metadata needed to verify your subscription. RevenueCat does not receive your custom scripts, profile, practice statistics, or camera content.
